Our Story

A Great Story Starts with a One Man

Anbu Raghavan was a man with a vision. He had always been interested in technology and saw the potential it had to change the world. After completing his education, he decided to put his knowledge and passion to good use by starting his own IT solutions company, Bumble Bees IT Solutions.

At first, it was a small operation, with just Anbu and a few employees working out of a small office. But Anbu was determined to make it succeed. He spent long hours working on projects and building relationships with clients, and slowly but surely, the company began to grow.

As the company grew, Anbu began to expand his team and hire more employees. He was always on the lookout for talented individuals who shared his passion for technology and had the skills and drive to help the company succeed. Today, Anbu is proud of all that he and his team have accomplished at Bumble Bees IT Solutions. From its humble beginnings, the company has grown into a thriving and successful business, and Anbu is grateful to have been a part of it all.
